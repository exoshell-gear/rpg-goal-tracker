HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

Right-click → Add new milestone

Alt/Option + click & drag → Move milestone or Avatar

Veronium is a goal-tracking system built to help you accomplish goals that would otherwise feel impossible.

Fight dragons, earn rewards, and accomplish your goals.

Step 1 — Add the Dragon

Right-click on the map → choose Dragon → place it at the end of the path.

The dragon is your big goal. It should feel scary, but not impossible.

A good dragon usually takes 3–6 months.

Example: Lose 20 lbs

Step 2 — Set the Treasure

When you place a dragon, a Treasure (trophy) appears automatically.

Click the treasure and write down how you will reward yourself after you defeat the dragon.

This should be something you normally would not do for yourself.

Examples: A trip to Mexico or a new watch

Step 3 — Add Your Starting Point

Now add a Start milestone at the beginning of the path.

This is where you are right now. Be honest.

Examples:

  • 20 lbs overweight
  • No gym membership yet
  • Idea only, no action taken

Step 4 — Build the Path

Now fill in the space between the start and the dragon with milestones.

The easiest way is to work backwards from the dragon.

Ask yourself:

“What has to happen before I can reach this?”

Each answer becomes a milestone.

Example:

  • Lose 20 lbs (Dragon)
  • Workout 4X/week for 3 months
  • Get a diet plan
  • Get a workout plan
  • Get a gym membership
  • 20 lbs overweight (Start)

Milestones vs Enemies

Milestones are normal steps.

Use them for things that need to be done, but aren’t scary.

Examples:

  • Get a gym membership
  • Get a workout plan

Enemies are things you are avoiding.

Use an enemy when the task is something you:

  • Don’t want to do
  • Keep putting off
  • Feel nervous about

Examples:

  • Your first day at the gym
  • Throwing out all junk food

Rewards

When you add an enemy, a small reward appears automatically. If you are like me, you probably forget to reward yourself.

These rewards should be:

  • Small
  • Fun
  • Guilt-free

Examples:

  • Watch your favorite show
  • Solo night out

Tasks Inside a Milestone

Tasks are things that must be done before you move forward.

Example:

You can’t go to the gym before you get a gym membership.

Moving Your Character

Your character represents you. Move your character when:

  • You complete a milestone
  • You defeat an enemy
  • You want to recognize progress

Why This Works

Veronium lets you break down difficult goals into smaller milestones, making them feel within reach.

It turns your life into a video game with checkpoints and rewards you for defeating enemies.
